HOW WE MET

Back in 2010, we met in a band called 'No Clue.' We were just musicians trying to make the most of life and truly enjoy, but we found something even more special - an amazing friendship. Laughing, playing music, and spending lots of time together made us best friends.
We didn’t realize it at first, but our friendship slowly turned into something magical.

It took a couple of years before we admitted our feelings. Then, on September 25th (which also happens to be her birthday), we knew it was something real and lucky for both of us.

Over the years, we went on adventures, explored new places, and shared lots of laughter, most of them with our darling nugget by our side. Our friendship grew deeper, and after more than a decade together, we’re excited to take the next big step and get married.

THE PROPOSAL

In the mesmerizing Land of Fire and Ice, amongst a series of thrilling adventures that we had already been on, there was one special surprise that he had planned.

For her, it was just another incredible escapade, filled with breathtaking sights and memorable moments. Little did she know that amidst the scenic beauty, something beautiful was about to unfold. As we stopped at picturesque spots to capture the beauty of Iceland, the last stop was handpicked by him. Amidst some awkward dancing and posing for photos, he took her hand and began to speak. The look in his eyes said it all - the moment she had dreamt of was finally here.

In the whirlwind of emotions that surrounded us when he got down on his knee, It was a blur of overwhelming happiness and realization. The proposal she had always imagined surpassed her wildest expectations. Her heart overflowed with joy and love as he asked the question, making it an incredibly intimate and cherished moment. She might be biased, but she believes it was the best proposal ever.

For him, it was a culmination of plotting and planning with their families, aiming for the ultimate surprise in one of the world's most breathtaking locations. The moment arrived in a perfect setting, amidst Iceland's stunning landscapes and their shared adventures. Seeing the joy and surprise in her eyes when she realized what was happening made every moment of preparation worthwhile. It was an intimate and heartfelt proposal, a moment they both will forever hold dear.

THE FUTURE

Our shared vision for the future is simple yet incredibly fulfilling—more laughter, lots of love, and crazy adventures.
We see our journey ahead filled with moments of joy, deep connections, and thrilling escapades.

Each day, we look forward to embracing life together, cherishing the moments that make us laugh uncontrollably, the love that binds us stronger, and the adventures that fuel our spirits.
