Accomodations

The best options closest to us!

AL FALAJ HOTEL

Wedding Reception venue. Best location as it’s also opposite the Church and about 2 minutes away from the Groom’s house! Book via booking.com or scout through online websites to find the best deals.

HAFFA HOUSE HOTEL

Also quite close to the Church, Reception Venue and the Groom’s Home! Great spacious rooms, and a great option to stay at!

AL QURUM RESORT

This boutique resort on Qurum beachfront offers air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony and views of Muscat or Arabian Gulf. About 10 minutes away from the wedding venues!

GOLDEN TULIP HEADINGTON

Located in the heart of Ruwi High street. Midpoint between Bride and Groom’s Homes! Less than 10 minutes away from the Wedding Venues.

SHOROUQ HOTEL APARTMENTS

Convenient Hotel Apartments that include a well-equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, an oven, and a stove top. Budget friendly, and about 5 minutes away from the Groom’s Home and Wedding Venues!
